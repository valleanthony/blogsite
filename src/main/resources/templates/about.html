About the Program :

The Java Programmer bootcamp offers a task-oriented, cost effective approach to learning Java technology. Our unique approach combines on-demand streaming with personal facilitation and comprehensive hands-on exercises to assure successful training and competence in the IT workplace Our classes are designed to be 50% lecture and 50% hands on exercises. An facilitator will be assigned to you to assist you in completing lab exercises and to help with any questions about the course content.
